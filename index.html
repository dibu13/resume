Unthrone

The game has been developed entirely during the second quarter of our university, (CITM, UPC) as the subject “project 3”. And it has been supervised by professors Ricard Pillosu & Marc Ripoll. The team was formed by 35 people, of which 17 were programmers, 10 Artists, 7 Designers and one producer.

The development of the game has been done with the engine: Culverin Engine. This has been developed in parallel with the development of the game. In the workflow, we followed a Scrum-Agile methodology, where all the members of the team have been at least Scrum Master once.

GhostFinder

Is a game developed in 48 hours for the Global Game Jam 2017 event.

In the game you are a ghost hunter that were hired to take pictures of ghosts, the objective is get the maximum amount of points taking quality photos of the ghosts in 3 minutes. Ghosts that are of different colors are only visible when you focus them with the flashlight, but are only clear using the flashlight with the complementary color of the ghost. Also the intensity of light fluctuates. With these two variables you have to take the photo with the light of the complementary color and the greater intensity of light to get more points.
